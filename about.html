Sharda Musical was founded in 1985 with a simple mission: to bring authentic, premium musical instruments to musicians and music lovers across India.

Over the past 40 years, we have built our reputation on three core pillars: authenticity, quality, and expertise.

Today, Sharda Musical stands as a beacon for musicians seeking premium instruments and unparalleled service.
